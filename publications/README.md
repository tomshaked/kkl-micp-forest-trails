# Publications and Intellectual Property

Research outputs — journal articles, conference presentations, and patent filings — produced during the project (Phases 4–5, Year 1 Q4 and Year 2 Q4).

---

## Target Outputs

### Journal Articles
Peer-reviewed papers submitted to Q1-ranked journals in geotechnical engineering, construction materials, and computational design. Planned topics:

- MICP biostimulation of Israeli forest soils: characterization and mechanical performance
- Robotic application of MICP for trail surface stabilization: protocols and field results
- 3D printing of integrated trail elements from MICP-stabilized local soil

### Conference Presentations
Presentation of interim and final results at relevant international venues, including:
- IASS (International Association for Shell and Spatial Structures)
- eCAADe (Education and Research in Computer Aided Architectural Design in Europe)
- Geotechnical and geoenvironmental engineering conferences

### Patents
Patent filings related to innovations developed during the project, including:
- Robotic MICP spraying protocols and equipment configurations
- 3D printing workflows for MICP-treated soil in infrastructure applications
